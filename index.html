EU4 Custom Nation Web Designer

Welcome!

Small site intended for planning out National Ideas for custom nations on Paradox Interactive's Europa Universalis IV game. It's hard to plan national ideas out of the blue because costs are not consistent, and are modified by the number of ideas you have on a particular area and the idea level. Initially, I only plan for this to work on-site, ephemerally. Later on, as I find time to work on it, I hope to add a save/load option (hopefully working directly with EU4's custom nation files so you can import directly into the game) and probably a sharing option that doesn't need sending anyone your exported custom nation file and just a link. Hope you enjoy it! - ccjmk

How to Use

By default, 200 points are assigned to use; you can customize the value if you want. Reserved points are for considering those to be used on tech-level, government or provinces. Select from each dropdown menu the idea you want and it will show you the level 1 modifier and cost; use the buttons on the right side to change the idea level. On the far-right side, you can see your current idea level count, for not going over 50% on any type set (as it gives you a heavy penalty on idea costs)

Suggestions & Bugs

If you have a suggestion, or you found out that some idea cost / name is incorrect, please let me know! Send me a PM on Reddit, I'll be glad to answer ASAP.
